In this, our second issue of the academic year, The Quad Review gathers student scientific writing drawn from laboratory notebooks, field observation, and independent research. We have asked our contributors — all current juniors and seniors — to translate their work into prose intelligible to any curious reader. The result, we believe, speaks for itself.

Science, at its best, is a communal act of paying attention. What follows is attention — careful, patient, occasionally humorous — directed at the quiet workings of the natural world.

— The Editors
