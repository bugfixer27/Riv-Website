Press Releases Student Bios Mentors

The Zygote is Riverdale's science research lab and the name of the course's research publication. We are a group of high school students in Riverdale's Science Research and Informatics course interested in learning how to experiment with and analyze large datasets using code.

As a class, we conduct experiments in biology and ecology. Recently, we wrote press releases summarizing new research throughout many fields of science. In the future, our site will serve as a place for Science Research and Informatics classes to present their findings to the broader Riverdale community.
