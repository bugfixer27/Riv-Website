In this issue, The Zygote gathers scientific writing from students of Science Research and Informatics, drawn from journal articles, field observation, and independent research. Our contributors translate current science into clear prose for any curious reader.

Science, at its best, is a communal act of paying attention. What follows is attention directed at the workings of coasts, moons, algorithms, public health, medicine, and the wider natural world.

- The Editors
